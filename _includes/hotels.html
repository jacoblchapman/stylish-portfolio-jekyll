Suggested Hotels

Here are a selection of hotels that have been recommended, not too far from the venue. Please feel free to look elsewhere, from our experience the best deals are on airbnb.

Casa Henrietta

A lovely hotel in beautiful Jimena with lots of character and an excellent base for trips to the surrounded area: Gibraltar, Málaga, Tarifa, Marbella. A good base to enjoy a relaxing holiday in the charm of a typical Spanish village.

Hostal Anon

This hotel is made up of several houses joined together making a quirky collection of patios, staircases, rooms and suites. You might spend the first day getting lost inside.

Hotel Almenara

The Hotel Almenara is in a peaceful green location in exclusive Sotogrande. It’s set on a hill in the heart of a golf course with great views across to the Mediterranean. A short drive takes you to the marina, which is lined with bars and restaurants.

Club Maritimo de Sotogrande

This property is 3 minutes walk from the beach. A beautiful hotel situated by the Sotogrande Marina overlooking Gibraltar, Morocco and the Mediterranean Sea, alongside some of the best golf courses in Europe.
